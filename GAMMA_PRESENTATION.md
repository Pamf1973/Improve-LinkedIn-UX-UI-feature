# MatchPoint — Swipe. Track. Land the Job.

---

## The Problem

Job hunting is exhausting. Boards are cluttered, applications vanish into a black hole, and there's no easy way to track where you stand.

You apply to 50 jobs. Then what? Spreadsheets? Sticky notes? Forgetting which recruiter emailed you back?

**Job seekers deserve better.**

---

## Meet MatchPoint

A swipe-based job discovery platform that makes finding, saving, and tracking jobs feel **effortless**.

- Real listings from Remotive, Jobicy & RemoteOK
- Swipe right to save, left to skip, down to archive
- One fluid motion. Zero friction.

---

## Discover in Seconds

Your job stack is personalized based on your skills and preferences.

- Skill-based match scoring shows you relevant jobs first
- Filter by type, salary, recency
- Search across 3 live APIs instantly
- Keyboard shortcuts for power users

**Feel:** Like flipping through a curated deck of opportunities made just for you.

---

## Save & Track Your Pipeline

Every saved job becomes part of your personal application pipeline.

**6 status stages:**
Saved > Applied > Interviewing > Offered > Accepted (or Rejected)

- One dropdown to update your status
- Color-coded badges everywhere so you always know where things stand
- Filter your saved jobs by status instantly

**Feel:** Total clarity — like having a personal recruiter organizing your search.

---

## Stay in Touch

Follow up without leaving MatchPoint.

- **Email** button pre-fills a professional follow-up template — just add the recipient
- **Contact** button searches for the company's careers page in one click
- **View** lets you revisit the job posting with zero side effects — no accidental status changes

**Feel:** Confident and proactive — every tool at your fingertips.

---

## Take Notes, Stay Sharp

Collapsed notes on every saved job card.

- Jot down the recruiter's name, interview date, prep reminders
- Auto-saves on blur, persists across sessions
- 500 characters — enough for what matters, not enough to overthink

**Feel:** Organized without effort — your memory, extended.

---

## Skip with Purpose

When you remove a skipped job, tell us why.

- Not relevant / Salary too low / Wrong location
- Don't meet requirements / Not interested in company / Other

This helps us (and you) understand your preferences better over time.

**Feel:** Heard. Your feedback shapes your future recommendations.
